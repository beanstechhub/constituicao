## What I built: constituicao.tech

An open-source platform that protects documents processed by AI systems. Each module maps to a Brazilian constitutional article — "the constitution of the digital world."

| Module | Protects | Constitutional basis | Tech |
|--------|----------|---------------------|------|
| **1. Prompt Injection Detection** | Documents processed by LLMs | Art. 5 LIV (due process) | 11 categories, 5-layer pipeline, OWASP LLM01 aligned |
| **2. Stylometric Integrity** | Academic authorship | Art. 205-214 (education rights) | 10 statistical features, local-only, no API dependency |
| **3. Digital Signature Verification** | PDF cryptographic authenticity | Art. 19 (public faith) | 3-layer validation (pyhanko + pypdf + heuristics) |
| **4. Fraud Shield** | Financial transactions | Art. 170 (economic order) | Go engine, 12 rules, ~100us latency, zero deps |

## What Claude specifically built

Every line of code in this platform was written in collaboration with Claude Opus 4.5/4.6:
